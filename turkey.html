Thanksgiving Turkey

Introduction

This recipe has been passed generation through generation, and a tradition in our family. Its been the gem of our holiday table, bringing everyone together to share this special day! This recipe will guide you while making the perfect Thanksgiving turkey.

Family Quote!

Every Thanksgiving, year to year, our family recipies have changed, we have tried so many new things, changing it up every year, but our crispy perfect golden turkey always stays with us making it all even better!

Tips, Ticks and Substitutions

  • Marinate the turkey overnight to make sure it stays juicy and flavorful.
  • Use a mixture of butter and fresh herbs under the skin for extra flavor.
  • Let the turkey rest for at least 20 mins before carving to retain its juices.
  • Put a whole orange inside the cleared out turkey while marinating and baking, this will make your turkey even more juicy.
  • If you have a roasting rack, use large chunks of vegetables (like onions, or garlic) to prop up the turkey.

Aya's Turkey Recipe
Recipe
Ingredient Quantity Notes Time
Whole Turkey 12-15 lbs Fresh or frozen (thawed) If frozen,let it sit for 5 hours
Butter 1/2 cup (melted) Use unsalted butter for better seasoning. No time needed.
Garlic powder 1 tablespoons Add 2 tablespoons if you want more flavor. Marinate for at least 2 hours(better overnight).
Salt and pepper Prefrence No Notes Marinate with garlic powder for at least 2 hours(better overnight)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 325^F (165^C).
  2. Pat the turkey dry and season inside and out with your seasoning and butter(dont use all of your butter), marinate overnight
  3. Bake vegetables with seasoning and olive oil seperately as a side for your turkey(pumpkin, onions, sweet pepper, carrots)
  4. Place turkey in a roasting pan and brush with leftover melted butter.
  5. Check internal temperature of the turkey(it should be 165^F in the thickest part).
  6. Put a cut up(in big chunks) orange, or full pineapple inside the turkey while baking.
  7. After putting in the turkey in the oven, bake for 3-4 hours while cheking up on it
  8. Let the turkey sit for 20 mins before serving
  9. Enjoy!
