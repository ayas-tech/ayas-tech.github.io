Thanksgiving Raspberry Pie

Introduction

This recipe is very unique,since not everyone knows it but from where my family is from its a very common thing, its been a part of our holiday table for a while now, bringing everyone a smile on their faces since its such a nice sweet treat. So lets share this special recipe on this special day! This recipe will guide you while making the perfect Thanksgiving raspberry pie.

Family Quote!

Thanksgiving is about family and the little things in it, like a sweet joy of raspberry pie bringing everyone together, one slice at a time!

Tips, Ticks and Substitutions

  • Get some fresh raspberries, but frozen raspberries are a great alternative.
  • Bake a regular crust for a few mins like for any pie, before adding the filling.
  • Sprinkle a thin layer of crust leftovers, or crushed cookies on the crust to absorb extra juice.
  • Let the pie cool completely before slicing for best experience.
  • You can use honey as an alternative for sugar, as a natural sweetness (although sugar tastes better).

Aya's Raspberry Pie Recipe
Recipe
Ingredient Quantity Notes
Fresh or frozen raspberries 4 cup Fresh perfered, but frozen will work too, just make sure there not too frozen
Sugar or honey 3/4 cup of sugar or honey Adjust based on taste
Cornstarch 3 tablespoons None.
Lemon Juice 1 tablespoon Adds brightness and enhances raspberry flavor
Whipped Cream Adjust to taste Prefrence

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375^F (190^C).
  2. In a bowl mix your raspberries, sugar, cornstarch and lemon juice until its an even supstance
  3. Pour the raspberry mixture into your prepared pie crust
  4. You can use store bought pie crust, but if your making one yourself follow the directions bellow.
  5. Roll your dough and fit it into a 9-inch pan, chill in the refrigerator.
  6. After your mixture is in the pie crust, add the top crust and seal the edges.
  7. Get a silicone bristle brush, and using it drag some butter on the pie, this makes it more shiny(Optional)
  8. Bake for 45-50 mins, or until the pie is golden brown
  9. Let the pie sit for 2-3 hours before slicing to allow the filling to set
  10. Enjoy!
